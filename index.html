Welcome to the GridFix documentation!¶

GridFix is a Python toolbox to facilitate preprocessing of scene fixation data for the grid-based analysis approach using Generalized Linear Mixed Models (GLMM) described in Nuthmann, Einhäuser & Schütz (2017) and Nuthmann & Einhäuser (2015).
